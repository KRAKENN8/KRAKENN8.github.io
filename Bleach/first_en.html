Memories of Nobody

rating: 7.5 / 10 year: 2008 director: Noriyuki Abe

Description of Memories of Nobody

Strange souls called "Blanks," which do not belong to either the human world or the spirit world, begin appearing in Karakura. Ichigo Kurosaki and Rukia Kuchiki meet a mysterious shinigami named Senna, who seems to have knowledge about what is happening. Together, they must fight against a dangerous group that seeks to disrupt the balance between worlds and destroy both the human world and the Soul Society.
