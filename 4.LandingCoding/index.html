    What lies beneath the surface:

    Dive into the fractured psyche of a mind struggling to make sense of itself. Through surreal visuals and unsettling moments, we unravel layers of memory, fear, and fleeting glimpses of hope, one thought at a time.

    Features of the experience
    • Narrative Exploration: Unearth fragmented memories and cryptic emotions.
    • Surreal Visuals: A haunting yet beautiful representation of a mind unravelling.
    • Immersive Atmosphere: Soundscapes and imagery that blur the lines between reality and dream.

    With each step, you'll piece together the story of a character navigating the fragile boundaries of their mind. The choices you make and the emotions you uncover are all part of an intimate, unforgettable journey.
    Dare to venture deeper and discover what lies beyond the milk.
